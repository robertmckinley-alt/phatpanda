— The founders

Built by two people.

Katrina and Rob McKinley founded Phat Panda in 2014 with a shared bet: cannabis quality and cannabis scale aren't mutually exclusive. A decade in, the original 30-person crew has grown to 850+ employees across three states.

Katrina McKinley

Co-Founder & CEO

Katrina is the co-founder and Chief Executive of Phat Panda. She's the architect of the company's brand world — from the original wordmark to the entire sub-brand family (Dabstract, Hot Sugar, Bangers, Snickle Fritz, Cake House, Kandy Shoppe, and more) — and steers the operation through multi-state expansion, regulatory shifts, and a quickly-changing industry.

She's been featured in Cannabis Business Times' founders profile and is a frequent voice on the operational and cultural challenges of scaling a cannabis company while keeping family-business DNA intact.

Rob McKinley

Co-Founder

Rob is the co-founder and operating force behind Phat Panda and Grow Op Farms, Washington's #1-selling Tier 3 cannabis producer. He has been named to the Green Entrepreneur Cannabis 100 and High Times 100 Most Influential People in Cannabis, and helped lead the company through Cannabis Cup, DOPE Cup, and Inlander Best of NW wins.

Before cannabis, Rob ran multi-state operations across hospitality and consumer goods. He brings that operator's lens to a notoriously fragmented industry — pairing craft cultivation with the Bamboo seed-to-sale platform, in-house extraction, and an in-house edibles kitchen.

— Boilerplate copy

Use this in your article.

Pre-approved descriptions you can paste directly into coverage. Updated quarterly.

Short version (≈30 words)

Phat Panda is a vertically-integrated cannabis company founded in 2014 by Katrina and Rob McKinley, ranked #1 Washington producer seven consecutive years (2019–2025) with a family of 12 brands across WA, CA, and MA.

Long version (≈90 words)

Phat Panda is one of the Pacific Northwest's largest and most-awarded cannabis producers. Founded in 2014 in Spokane Valley, Washington by Rob and Katrina McKinley, the company grew from a 30-person crew into a 850+ employee operation spanning Washington, California, and Massachusetts. Its family of 12 brands — including Dabstract, Hot Sugar, Sticky Frog, Bangers, Snickle Fritz, Panda Pen, Cake House, Kandy Shoppe, Eluzion, Hot Shotz, Juice Box, and Firecracker — has won multiple Cannabis Cups, DOPE Cups, and reader-voted "Best of" titles, with founders named to High Times and Green Entrepreneur influence lists.
