— Who we are

From a 30-Panda crew to a family of 550+.

Phat Panda was founded in 2014 by Robert and Katrina McKinley with a simple mission — bring the highest-quality, most affordable cannabis to market by combining advanced cultivation with state-of-the-art technology.

A decade later, we're the parent of a multi-state family of brands, recognized as one of the top cannabis producers in the Pacific Northwest, and home to one of the most loyal customer bases in the industry. We grow, extract, package, and ship every product under one roof — built by people who actually use what they make.

This is craft, at scale.
