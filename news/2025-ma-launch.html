Nov 2025 Expansion

We're now live in Massachusetts.

Eleven years after planting the first room in Spokane Valley, the Panda family officially crosses to the East Coast. As of November 2025, Phat Panda flower, Hot Sugar edibles, Dabstract concentrates, and select Panda Pen vapes are available at licensed adult-use dispensaries across Massachusetts.

What's available

  • Phat Panda flower — eighths and pre-rolls of our top-selling strains, grown to the same single-strain, hand-trimmed standard as the WA flagship line.
  • Hot Sugar edibles — gummies in MA-compliant 5mg single-serving doses, made in our in-house kitchen.
  • Dabstract concentrates — live rosin, cured resin, and live-resin cartridges across the most-requested strain rotation.
  • Panda Pen — disposable and 510-thread live-resin carts.

Why Massachusetts

Massachusetts has one of the most demanding regulatory regimes in the country. Every batch is independently tested before it leaves manufacturing. Packaging is locked down with universal symbols and pediatric copy. Dosing is capped at 5mg per serving, 100mg per package. The compliance cost of operating there is real.

It's also one of the most discerning consumer markets we've seen. East-coast cannabis culture grew up on Northeast-grown craft flower — Vermont, Massachusetts, upstate New York — and customers know the difference. So when we say we're going to ship our best work to MA shelves, we mean it. The expectations are right where they should be.

Massachusetts gets a curated cut, not a watered-down version. Same rooms, same hands, same standards.

Where to find it

Initial launch partners are concentrated in Greater Boston, the Cape, and Worcester County, with broader distribution rolling out through Q1 2026. We're building a Massachusetts version of our retailer locator now — for the latest store list, follow @phatpanda on Instagram or ask your local budtender.

Welcome, Massachusetts. We're glad to be here.
