Sep 2024 Awards

Inlander Best of NW: Best Cannabis Brand, again.

Spokane-region readers picked Phat Panda for Best Cannabis Brand in The Inlander's 2024 Best of the Inland Northwest awards — the second consecutive year. Reader-voted, which means it's not a panel or an industry-insider call. It's the people who actually buy the product.

To the budtenders who recommend us, the retailers who stock the deep cut SKUs, and every customer who voted: thank you. Spokane has been home base since 2014, and the hometown win means more than the trade-press recognition. You built this with us.

What it actually rewards

Reader-voted "Best Of" awards have a useful filter on them — you can't buy your way in. They reward the things customers actually notice over the course of a year: does the flower stay fresh?, do the carts work?, is the eighth I bought last Tuesday the same eighth I bought three months ago?, does the budtender mention you when I ask what's good?

It's also a useful pressure check on us. A second consecutive win means we kept the bar where it was; if we slip, you'll let us know on the ballot.

Hometown awards are the hardest ones to win twice. You can fool a panel once. You can't fool the people who live here.

What's next

We have a few new strains rolling out for fall, a Snickle Fritz refresh that drops in October, and the long-promised Cake House holiday line landing in late November. Three states, twelve brands, the same family.
